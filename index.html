MY STORY

Hi, I am Shivam Tiwari.I initially started working out to maintain my physique and to look good like every other youngster in the society. Hitting the gym regularly made me realize that it is a part of my life. I enjoyed every workout sessions and in the process it actually became an addiction. I realized that If a person like me can transform his body then anyone can do it if guided properly, that very moment I realized that the purpose of my existence is to promote fitness.

SHIVAM FITNESS was formed with one prime vision to encourage and inspire people to understand their inherent beauty of fitness and wellness, by adapting healthy practices in their day to day life. My passion in the field of fitness led me to join this industry. Our motive is to deliver innovative fitness programs. We will inspire and support you in your commitment towards enhancing the quality of your life.
